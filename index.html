Privacy Policy

GITAM AutoCaptcha Extension

Last Updated: July 16, 2025

Privacy-First Approach: This extension stores your GITAM login credentials locally on your device only. We do not collect, access, or store any data on our servers.

1. Information Handling

The GITAM AutoCaptcha extension may store the following information locally on your device:

  • GITAM portal username and password (only if you choose to save them)
  • Extension settings and user preferences

2. Local Storage Architecture

All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's secure storage API. This ensures:

  • ✓ Data remains exclusively on your device
  • ✓ We cannot access your stored credentials
  • ✓ No data transmission to external servers
  • ✓ Zero data sharing with third parties

3. Data Usage

The stored credentials are used exclusively for:

  • Auto-filling login forms on GITAM educational portals
  • Automated captcha solving for seamless access
  • Enhanced user experience and login efficiency

4. Security Measures

Your data is protected through multiple layers:

  • Chrome's built-in security and encryption mechanisms
  • Local storage encryption protocols
  • Zero network transmission of sensitive credentials
  • Sandboxed extension environment

5. User Control & Data Management

You maintain complete control over your data and can delete it at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the extension from Chrome
  • Clearing browser data specific to the extension
  • Using the extension's built-in settings to clear saved credentials
  • Resetting Chrome's extension data through browser settings

6. Privacy Commitment

This extension maintains strict privacy standards and does not:

  • ✗ Use analytics or tracking services
  • ✗ Monitor your browsing behavior
  • ✗ Send usage data to external servers
  • ✗ Use cookies for tracking purposes
  • ✗ Collect personal information beyond login credentials
